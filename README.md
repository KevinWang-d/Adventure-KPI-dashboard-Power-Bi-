# Adventure-KPI-dashboard-Power-Bi-

Overview
This project is an interactive KPI dashboard built in Power BI to monitor business performance and support data-driven decision making.

Business Objective
Track key performance indicators (KPIs)
Monitor revenue, orders, and returns
Support executive reporting

Business Questions
What are the top cities they ship to and the sales per city
Who are the top customers they sell to.
Who are their top sales agents

Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Data Cleaning

What I Did
- Cleaned and prepared raw sales data
- Built data relationships and measures
- Created KPI cards and trend analysis
- Designed interactive visuals and slicers
- Built executive summary pages

Key Insights
- Monthly revenue reached $1.83M, exceeding target
- Accessories and bikes drive the highest order volume
- Water Bottle (30 oz) is the top-selling product
- Regional performance varies across markets
